The Steps

EXPLORING THE LANGUAGE TRAP

Recovering experiential language- Disrupting the schooled perception and breaking the conditioned cognitive habits - An orientation program to recover the ability to learn the real WORLD rather than the word by addressing the habits instilled by the 15 odd years of schooling.

AWAKENING SENSES, AWAKENING THE SENSE OF AESTHETICS

Awakening the aesthetic sense than learning the western aesthetics- connecting with the natural beauty around or developing contextual aesthetic sense.

LEARNING FROM MATERIALS/ CONTEXTS.

Exploring materials/ objects/ artifacts etc. to understand the core quality of materials the way children explore the world.

LEARNING FROM NATURE - PROCESS ORIENTATION

To awaken the 'sense of process' which is the backbone of the natural learning process.

LEARNING FROM THE ILLITERATES, LEARNING FROM CHILDREN

Seeing the world with fresh eyes- creativity and innovation among rural tribal communities

FACING THE WORLD

Understanding the modern crisis- ecology, alienation, anthropocentrism
