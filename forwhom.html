REDEFINING ROLES

ARTISTS

who want to understand how the present notions of art are detrimental to the natural awakening of our authentic aesthetic sense.

DESIGNERS

who want to develop sustainable solutions and to move from product-oriented design to process-orientation.

PROFESSIONALS

involved in child-related work like writing stories, books, poetry, toy-making, art class, pedagogy making etc. who wish to move beyond the infantilizing of children, which instead of helping, damages children. 

LITERACY ACTIVISTS

to understand how introduction of literacy in early age damages our natural cognitive system meant for creating knowledge and numbs our senses.

PLAY WORKERS,

who really love children, to understand they are the most detrimental to children’s natural play and their very existence is a total disrespect to the very nature of play.

SOCIAL WORKERS

who really want to understand the politics of modern social work and how the act of help disrespects the person being helped.
